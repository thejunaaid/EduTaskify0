Junaid Ahmad Malik
Creator · Developer · EduTaskify
📍 Anantnag, J&K, India @thejunaaid

Hi, I'm Junaid Ahmad Malik, a passionate developer and student from Anantnag, Jammu & Kashmir. I built EduTaskify because I wanted a tool that truly understood the journey of competitive exam aspirants — the long study hours, the fear of falling behind, and the small wins that keep you going. Every pixel of this app was crafted with love, frustration, and a whole lot of chai.

EduTaskify is my answer to the chaos of student life. Instead of scattered notes and forgotten goals, you get a cosmic command center — habit tracking, a Pomodoro timer, full syllabus management, XP levels, streak protection, leaderboards, certificates, and much more. I wanted students to feel like heroes of their own study journey, not just numbers on a marksheet. Every feature was designed with that purpose: to make studying feel purposeful, gamified, and actually rewarding.

This app is completely free, and I plan to keep it that way. I believe quality study tools shouldn't be behind a paywall — especially for students across India who deserve the best. If EduTaskify helps you crack NEET, JEE, UPSC, or ace your boards, that's my biggest reward. Keep studying hard, stay consistent, and remember — every great rank starts with one great habit. 🚀
